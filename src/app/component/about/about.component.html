About Akashaj Cold Pressed Oil Company

Akashaj is an Indian company which is leading specialist manufacturer of high quality cold pressed oils, using fresh Indian grown produce. With a diverse and unique range of products, we provide customers with consistent, quality cold pressed oil products and services. Our company is focused on delivering complete value for money experience to the customers; hence, we offer only 100% natural products at economical prices.

Akashaj Oil believe in traditional farming and started producing own natural/organic cold pressed oils. Oils are extracted using traditional wood pressed method. No chemicals or preservatives were added and not undergone any refinement process. Only pure & premium natural wooden cold pressed cooking oils we provide to the customer.

We are a manufacturer and supplier of cold pressed groundnut oil, coconut oil, sesame oil, and many more all over Maharashtra. All the oils are made from the finest quality food-grade seeds sourced from farms in India. We take utmost care to preserve their true nutritional value, flavor and aroma.
